Privacy Policy – Sannivesham

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Sannivesham. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use the Sannivesham website and mobile application.

Information We Collect

Sannivesham is an educational platform that provides access to educational content through our website and mobile application.

We may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Browser type and version
  • App usage statistics
  • Performance and diagnostic data

This information is collected solely to improve the functionality, security, reliability, and user experience of our services.

Personal Information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information from users unless it is voluntarily provided through contact forms, registrations, feedback forms, or other features offered by our services.

Any personal information provided by users will only be used for the purpose for which it was submitted.

How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide educational content and services
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Monitor and maintain platform security
  • Prevent misuse and unauthorized activities
  • Respond to user inquiries, feedback, and support requests
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade users' personal information to third parties.

Information may be shared only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights, safety, or property
  • Required for the operation of services provided by trusted third-party partners

Third-Party Services

Our website and application may use third-party services such as:

  • Web hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs)
  • Cloud infrastructure providers

These services may collect and process technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Sannivesham is intended for educational purposes and may be used by students, teachers, and the general public.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental or guardian consent where required by applicable laws.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and security measures to help protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

External Links

Our services may contain links to external websites, educational resources, advertisements, social media platforms, and other third-party content that may be useful to our users.

All external links and third-party content included within Sannivesham are carefully reviewed and monitored before being made available to users. We strive to ensure that such content is relevant, safe, educational, and appropriate for our audience, including students and younger users.

We continuously work to maintain a positive and secure user experience and may update, modify, or remove external content whenever necessary to uphold our quality and safety standards.

Users are encouraged to use external resources responsibly and in accordance with the terms and policies of the respective platforms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Continued use of our services after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
